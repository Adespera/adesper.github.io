My Process

To Understand, To Research, To Craft

While starting any project I generally follow VIP method, i.e., Vision in Product Design.

  • Uncovering: Immersing myself in the project goals, user needs, and market context.
  • Exploring: Conducting thorough research to understand past, present, and future trends.
  • Creating: Developing innovative solutions that resonate with users and achieve project objectives.
